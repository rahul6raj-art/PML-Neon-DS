## Behaviour

- **Typography** — Goal title **Body** medium **14/20**; status **Subtext** **12/16**; **%** chip **Title 4** medium on **positive-weak** / **notice-weak** / **negative-weak** by tone; footer labels **Body** regular, amounts **Body** medium (**`--text-neutral-strong`**).
- **Progress** — **2px** track (**`--background-neutral-weak`**), gradient fill + thumb; **`progressPercent`** clamped **0–100**.
- **CTAs** — Optional **`primaryCtaLabel`** / **`secondaryCtaLabel`**; **`on*Press`** optional in product; clicks **`stopPropagation`** when the card is also pressable.
