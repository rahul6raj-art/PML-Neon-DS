## Playground

On the **Docs** tab, the **Controls** table lives in this section (below the canvas). **Playground** starts with every boolean **off** (no section header, no chevron, text moves, no status badges, no **top** icon/logo row, not pressable)—turn them on to match your layout. Expand **`items`** (object) for **`leadingLogoName`** / **`leadingIconName`**, **`changeSentiment`**, **`statusBadgeLabel`**, and per-tile overrides.
