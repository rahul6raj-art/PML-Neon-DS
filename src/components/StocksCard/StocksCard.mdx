## Playground

Use **Show status badge** to show or hide the top **notice** **Badge** (muted). When it is on, edit **statusLabel** (e.g. `Pledge`). Other controls map to the component props: title, quantity, average price line, LTP, and change line (with **changeSentiment** for colour).

## Other stories

## Content & tokens

- **Surface:** **`Card`** (default padding `--card-padding-*`, radius `--radius-24`, `--surface-level-1`).
- **Badge:** **`Badge`** `type="text"` **`context="notice"`** **`muted`** when `statusLabel` is set.
- **Typography:** **`body-medium`** (title), **`body-regular`** (price), **`subtext-regular`** (meta, change).
- **Colours:** **`--text-neutral-strong`**, **`--text-neutral-weak`** (bullet), **`--text-positive-strong`** / **`--text-negative-strong`** / **`--text-neutral-moderate`** (change).
- **Spacing:** **`--spacing-4`**, **`--spacing-8`**, **`--spacing-12`**, **`--spacing-16`**.
- **INR:** No space between **₹** and the amount in copy and props.
